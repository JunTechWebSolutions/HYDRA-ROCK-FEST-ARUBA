What is HYDRA ROCK FEST ARUBA

HYDRA ROCK FEST was established in 2017 as a platform for rock musicians of all ages and skill levels to perform, connect, and grow. That is why every year, HYDRA ROCK FEST is adjudicated by an esteemed panel of local and international professionals, ensuring a high standard of evaluation and meaningful feedback for every performer.
